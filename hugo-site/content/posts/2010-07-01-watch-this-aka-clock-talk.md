Watches, why not? Don't judge me. ![uber](/uploads/2010/07/uber.jpg "uber") Function plus style. Multitasking? You cannot go wrong with a watch. So, I have been poking around a bit, and at first, I had my eye on this [jacques lemans watch](http://www.bluedial.com/jacques-lemans-watch.htm) I found online at bluedial.com: ![boxy](/uploads/2010/07/j-lemons.jpg "j-lemons") Right? There still exists a soft spot in my heart for a good, old fashioned watch...maybe if the Incredible looked more robotic, that would cease to be the case.
